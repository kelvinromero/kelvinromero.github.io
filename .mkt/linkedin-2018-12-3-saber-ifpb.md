During my internship at IFPB, I built SABER — a search engine for researchers, projects, and competencies across the institution. Django, Elasticsearch, DRF.

The most interesting part wasn't the tech itself, but learning that full-text search isn't SQL LIKE, that data quality matters more than algorithms, and that search UX is its own discipline.

The project was registered as intellectual property with Brazil's INPI. I rewrote the article in English with more context: https://kelvinromero.github.io/2018/12/03/saber-ifpb/
